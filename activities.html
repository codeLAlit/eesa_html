Activities

Here is a glimpse of activities orgnised by us.

Department Freshers Orientation

We organised Orientation for department freshers. It was organised to educated them about the events and activities held in department throughout the year.
There were performances by sophomore and freshers. The event started with an introductory speech by Dept. General Secretary. In the event freshers interacted with their immediate seniors.

TT tournament 2019

We organised Table Tennis tournament at New SAC, IIT Bombay. Students from all years participated with full enthusiasm.
The event was organised to encourage the sports talent in our department. Winners were presented certificates.

Department Cultural Night

We organised the first ever cultural night of the department. The event held at LTPSCA, IIT Bombay.
Students performed solo songs, dances, band performances, duets, standup and poetry. The performances were mesmerizing. The event was a great opportunity for students to showcase their talent and refresh their mood.

Department Trek Pawana Lake

We organised a camping at Pawana Lake followed by trekking to top of Tikona Fort.
Students enjoyed the stay in the tent camps at Lakeside. The whole event was enjoying and students gave great response.

Department Kurta Day

Department organised Kurta Day. Teachers were felicitated for their extraordinary contribution in teaching.
Students came wearing traditional dress. Teachers shared their stories and interacted with the students.
